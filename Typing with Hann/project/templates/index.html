Game Description

Typing with Hann

You, a hero from other world, decide to venture across this unfamiliar world, where all your fantasy is real. Upon making your tent in the middle of a silent forest, you encounter a little girl who got lost there. After gaining her trust, she tell you her name, Hann. You try to interogate Hann with a friendly way, asking why's she here? Hann keeps stutering while answering, but deep inside you know that something is wrong. "Th..this fo-forest is da-dangerous, on-once you e-enter yo-u can't ge-get o-out". Hearing these words, you realise that both of you already trapped inside this dark dangerous forest and there's no way out. The mist around the forest has thickened as the sun goes down. The monster's footsteps, sounds can now be heard from all the corner. How long will you survive in this forest? Will you be able to escape safely with Hann?
